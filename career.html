graduate trainee program

Engro Enablers - (GTE) is an engineering leadership program
that entails of a 12-month immersion into technical aspects
of the business. It aims to align the expectations and
skill set of aspiring engineering graduates with the company’s
business needs. It builds a talent pipeline to groom Graduate
Trainee Engineers from within the organization for future
leadership roles. This continual absorption of high
potential graduates ensures that future leadership positions
in the company are filled by the most
outstanding talent.
